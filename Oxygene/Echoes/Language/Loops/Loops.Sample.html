Loops Sample

Purpose

The sample shows off all Oxygene loops constructions.

Examine the code

Look at the loopsTesting method to see loops constructions:
  • "for" loop, taking every 5th item
  • "for" loop, going from high to low value
  • "for each" loop with defined "index" variable
  • simple "loop" construct that loops endlessly, until broken out of
  • "repeat ... until" and "while ... do" loops
